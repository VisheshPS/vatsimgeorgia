On April 9, get ready for the second leg of the expedition of the Great Andaman Run from Chennai (VOMM) to Port Blair (VOPB) in the Andaman and Nicobar islands between 1200Z and 1630Z. Enjoy the view of the beautiful turquoise and dark blue colours along with the scenic approach into Port Blair.

So what are you waiting for? Get on board your favourite aircraft and join us for the Great Andaman Run event. Our controllers are excited to welcome you to Port Blair. #DiscoverIndiaDiscoverVATSIM

Event Details: 

Event Date – April 9, 2023 
Departure (VOMM) – 1200Z to 1430Z 
Arrival (VOPB) – 1330Z to 1630Z

Suggested Event Route:

SIDAT IDASO P761 LEGIN
